1. Drift from legible CoT due to further scaling up RL.
	- Inb4, RLHF uses human evaluation, which allows for legible CoT.
	- Now, most models use outcome-based reinforment learning, which does not incentivize legibility.
2. Direct supervision of CoT.
	- Models are usually only rewarded for correct outputs regardless of how they arrive at them -> their reasoning process is not subject to any optimization pressures.
	- There is also [[Process Supervision]]
